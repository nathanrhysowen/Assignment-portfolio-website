Final design:

In the first design, I decided to place both texts inside two circles as a form of repition.
I also warped the text however I scratched this idea in my final design because i thought
that the text was too hard to read which was a design flaw. I split the page diagonally and
made one half white and the other red because i like the contrast between the two colours.
The logo is also placed in the center to align it with the diagonal split since the logo is also
split in half.

For my second design i decided to split the page again but this time horizontally in the middle
and keep the white and red contrasting colour scheme. I curved the seperation line instead of
making it straight because it makes it look as if the red is paint oozing across the canvas. I
seperated the two texts with a diagonal line as a form of repitition similar to how the T and F
in the logo is seperated and aligned the text so that there is white space above and below it.

In my 3rd design i split the page horizontally yet again, however this time with a straight line
to see how it looked. What sets this design apart from the rest is the use of the spraypaint
effect behind the text wich is repeated for the title. I mirrored the colours of the text with the
spray paint behind to contrast the colours. I also alligned all the text within the center of the
page.

For my final design, I tried to incorporate ideas from all of my previous designs. For the title, I
repeated the colour scheme from the logo by making the "true" black and "false" white. In
addition, I also borrowed the spray paint idea from my previous design and spray painted red
behind the "false", mirroring the logo. I also used the curved line from my previous design to
further push the idea of paint. The poster is for a film festival and so the reference to paint fits
in my opinon, as both film and paint are linked with the art of creation. I split both texts with a
diagonal line repeating the pattern from the logo and alligned the text to follow the line. In all
of my designs I refrained from adding pictures, this is because I am a fan of minimalist design
and did not want the page to be too cluttered and would prefer it if there is more white space.
I stuck to only 2 contrasting colours to further push the minimilist design.

Strategy and Scope

The fundemental goal of this website is to display all of my tasks completed in this module in a way that is easy to read and nice to look at. In addition, another goal is to fulfill the assignment requirements to a standard that I, myself, am happy with. The users of the website are teachers, possibly parents, friends and me. When designing the website it is important to note the different users and how each user will navigate the page and react. The website will contain the CARP task, the coding tasks, the type task and UX task.
